# Privacy and Legal Note for Vehicle Photo Capture

- Vehicle images can contain personally identifiable information (plate numbers, faces, location context).
- Apply data minimization: capture only required tire/vehicle regions for inspection workflows.
- Implement automatic redaction for license plates and faces before sharing outside secured operations.
- Store captures encrypted at rest, with role-based access control and audit logs.
- Define retention windows and deletion policy aligned with local regulations and customer contracts.
- In public or semi-public spaces, verify consent/signage and legal basis for collection before deployment.
